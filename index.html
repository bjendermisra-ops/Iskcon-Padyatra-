ISKCON & Sankirtan Knowledge Hub

Explore authoritative insights into the philosophy, founder-acharya Srila Prabhupada, and the worldwide congregational chanting movement.

What is ISKCON and who is Srila Prabhupada?
ISKCON stands for the International Society for Krishna Consciousness, established in 1966 by His Divine Grace A.C. Bhaktivedanta Swami Prabhupada. Srila Prabhupada is the founder-acharya who brought the timeless wisdom of the Bhagavad-gita and the chanting of the Hare Krishna Maha-mantra from India to the global stage, establishing over 108 temples, farm communities, and schools globally.
What is the significance of the Harinam Sankirtan movement?
Harinam Sankirtan is the congregational chanting of the holy names of God, particularly the Hare Krishna maha-mantra: Hare Krishna, Hare Krishna, Krishna Krishna, Hare Hare / Hare Rama, Hare Rama, Rama Rama, Hare Hare. Sri Chaitanya Mahaprabhu introduced this movement in India over 500 years ago as the most effective spiritual practice for the current age (Kali-yuga) to awaken pure love of God in the hearts of all living entities.
How do I find an ISKCON Temple or Padyatra near me?
Our integrated ISKCON Padyatra mobile application provides global coordination coordinates. By enabling live GPS tracking, you can find the nearest walking pilgrimage (Padyatra), register your local temple attendance, access daily deity darshans, and coordinate directly with local temple presidents and Padyatra organizers worldwide.
What spiritual literature and Bhagavad-gita guides are distributed?
During every ISKCON Padyatra, devotees distribute thousands of authoritative spiritual translations written by Srila Prabhupada, including the **Bhagavad-gita As It Is**, Srimad-Bhagavatam, and numerous introductory booklets on Krishna consciousness. Our app keeps track of real-time book distribution metrics (currently over 4,400 books distributed) across all active routes.
